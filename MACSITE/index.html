cartivoice

2019cartivoice.com

=======================

Role: Concept, design, coding

carti voice is a meme generator that takes user-input text and translates it into the unique typing style of rapper Playboi Carti.

I came up with the idea one day in March of 2019, and four days later, the site was live. It went viral immediately after release, with thousands of Carti fans using the site to create tweets and Instagram captions. The site still receives thousands of page views weekly.

This Show Rules

2019thisshowrules.com

=======================

Role: Concept, design, coding

This Show Rules is a soundboard based on clips from Time Crisis, an Apple Music 1 radio show hosted by Vampire Weekend frontman Ezra Koenig & American painter Jake Longstreth.

Users can click on the different squares to play iconic “drops” from the show, including “8 Minute Cape Cod” and “Corporate Food History,” as well as classic quotes.

#HoodBabySurf

2019Download on iOS App Store

=======================

Role: iOS development, distribution

Client: Alamo Records

#HoodBabySurf is an iOS game where the player controls rapper Lil Gotit as he surfs the seas saving mermaids and collecting powerups.

The game features a soundtrack comprised of Lil Gotit's biggest hits, and is often updated to promote his new album and single releases. An iMessage sticker pack with Lil Gotit-related illustrations is included with the app.
