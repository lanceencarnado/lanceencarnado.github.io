About
         

Storytelling and good music epitomize the vision of AMCO. We are a distribution, publishing and management company committed to working with artists who captivate and stay true to their respective imagination.

AMCO is founded by the team behind renowned independent music collective, Awful Records, known for artists such as Father, Playboi Carti, Zack Fox, ABRA, Tommy Genesis, Faye Webster, and Danger Incorporated, amongst others.

         
AMCO IS A DISTRIBUTION, PUBLISHING AND MANAGEMENT COMPANY IN PARTNERSHIP WITH OPPOSITION/BROADBANDTV CORPORATION AND BECAUSE MUSIC GROUP.
